## How This Repository Uses The Sources

The local `seedance-cinematic-storyboard` skill is an original Chinese workflow distilled from recurring open-source patterns: timed shot breakdowns, explicit reference-role mapping, camera and lighting vocabulary, sound design, and long-video continuation. It intentionally avoids copying the upstream skill bodies wholesale.
